Pipeline Details

Audio Input: Raw WAV files from IEMOCAP or CREMA-D datasets
Preprocessing: Normalize audio amplitude, resample to 16 kHz
Feature Extraction: WavLM-base (768-dim) or HuBERT-large (1024-dim) embeddings
Pooling: Mean or max pooling over time steps to get fixed-size vector
Classification: Train MLP, SVM, or XGBoost on embeddings
Output: Emotion class (anger, sadness, happiness, neutral, etc.)
